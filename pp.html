Privacy Policy for Alexa GPT Skill

Effective Date: 31-07-2023

Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use the Alexa GPT skill ("the Skill"). The Skill is powered by OpenAI's GPT-3.5 architecture and integrated with the Alexa ecosystem to provide an enhanced conversational experience. By using the Skill, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Data Collection and Usage

  1. Personal Information: The Skill does not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII), such as your name, email address, or contact details.
  2. Conversational Data: During interactions with the Skill, your voice queries, responses, and other conversational data are temporarily processed to provide accurate and contextually relevant responses. However, this data is not associated with your identity and is not stored beyond the immediate session.
  3. Usage Analytics: We may collect aggregated, non-personal information about Skill usage, such as the number of interactions, session durations, and frequently used features. This data is used solely for improving the Skill's performance and user experience.

Data Disclosure

  1. Third-Party Services: We do not share your personal information with third-party services or entities.
  2. Legal Obligations: We may disclose information if required by law, to protect our rights, or to comply with a legal process.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to safeguard your information and maintain data security. However, please note that no method of data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is entirely secure.

Children's Privacy

The Skill is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will promptly delete it.
